ivid

Feature management that starts with the feature, not the flag.​

Vivid helps software teams control, release, and operate features with confidence. Instead of scattering isolated flags across your codebase, Vivid puts the feature itself at the center: turn it on, turn it off, enrich it with flags or metadata, and manage its behavior per environment.

Built for developers, product owners, business analysts, and support teams, Vivid makes feature control understandable for everyone involved in building and running software.

🎛️ Control features not just flags

Enable or disable entire features per environment. Add optional flags and metadata when you need more precise runtime behavior.

🤝 Built for the whole team

Give developers, product owners, analysts, and support teams a shared view of what is active, where it is active, and how it behaves.

🔐 Secure by design

Vivid uses OIDC for authentication and role-based access control to decide who can view, change, or manage each part of the system.

🌍 Environment-aware

Manage feature state independently across development, testing, staging, production, or any environment your team relies on.

🧩 Developer-friendly

Integrate with the Kotlin SDK and Spring Boot starter. Client integrations are actively evolving as Vivid grows.

🚀 Open source and beta-ready

Vivid is open source, free to use, and currently in beta. Built to become a production-ready feature management platform.

Core Features​

Your features in the spotlight – always stay on top of things
Be honest: Do you know off the top of your head which features are active in which stages? With Vivid’s feature overview, guesswork is a thing of the past. We provide you with a dashboard where you can see at a glance what’s happening where.No searching, no asking, just pure clarity for you and your team.
Precision, not a scattergun approach: Control your app down to the smallest detail.
Is a simple on/off switch not enough for you? It’s not enough for us either. With Vivid, you can map out complex workflows using granular sub-flags or attach metadata directly to your featuresWhether you want to customize a layout element only in Switzerland or need a special banner text for app version 2.0, you have the power to do so without having to redeploy a single line of code.
Full transparency: Keep track of your applications
Have you ever wondered which of your services or app versions is currently using which features? With Vivid, you no longer have to guess. We give you in-depth insights into your clients’ configurations. See in real time which application is connecting to which environment and what the actual status of your flag settings is there.It’s the ultimate safety net: You’ll immediately know if an instance is still running an outdated version or if a flag is interpreted unexpectedly. Maximum control over your distributed system landscape.
Vivid adapts to you, not the other way around.
Every company operates differently, and every deployment setup is unique. We don’t force you into a rigid framework. Whether you only use Dev and Prod or have a complex chain of five different staging environments, Vivid lets you map your environment exactly as it is. Quick to set up, intuitive to manage, and ready for your next big release.

Why Vivid?​

Feature flags are powerful, but they often become technical details hidden in code. Vivid turns them into manageable product capabilities.

With Vivid, teams can describe a feature once, control it across environments, and extend it with metadata whenever more advanced behavior is needed. That makes feature management easier to understand, safer to operate, and more useful beyond the development team.

Whether you are preparing a release, testing behavior in staging, supporting production users, or coordinating product decisions, Vivid gives your team a shared control surface for the features that matter.
