## Feature management that starts with the feature, not the flag.

Vivid helps software teams control, release, and operate features with confidence.
Instead of scattering isolated flags across your codebase, Vivid puts the **feature itself** at the center:
turn it on, turn it off, enrich it with flags or metadata, and manage its behavior per environment.

Built for developers, product owners, business analysts, and support teams, Vivid makes feature control understandable for everyone involved in building and running software.

## Why Vivid?

Feature flags are powerful, but they often become technical details hidden in code.
Vivid turns them into manageable product capabilities.

With Vivid, teams can describe a feature once, control it across environments, and extend it with metadata whenever more advanced behavior is needed.
That makes feature management easier to understand, safer to operate, and more useful beyond the development team.

Whether you are preparing a release, testing behavior in staging, supporting production users, or coordinating product decisions, Vivid gives your team a shared control surface for the features that matter.
